Meet our amazing panel of Speakers!

Sahil Sharma (DJ Zaeden)

EDM has taken over the lives of the 21st century kids. Everyday a teenager sits in front a laptop and attempts to produce a song of their own but there are very few who have the potential to take it to the next level. Sahil Sharma AKA Zaeden is a Heritage Alumni who made it into the competitive big leagues of electronic music. Being the youngest DJ and music producer to be featured in Tomorrowland alongside Borgeous, he has become a role model to those who were afraid of entering into the exciting field of EDM. Zaeden will inspire you with his story on how he managed to overcome the challenges and fear that he faced on the 26th of August 2016 at the very first TEDx of The Heritage School, Gurgaon.

Malavath Poorna

Climbing Mt. Everest is no small task. It takes dedication and patience. It seems like an impossible task to most of us. So how did Malavath Poorna, a 13 year old girl barely able to feed herself end up becoming the youngest woman to climb the indomitable Mount Everest? By working hard and not giving up. On her way to the summit Poorna saw 6 dead bodies.They were bodies of people having the same aspirations as she did. Poorna focused on her goals and when it was the hardest, she hit the refresh button on her line of thought and continued forwards with a rejuvenated sense of determination. Upon reaching the top, she raised the flag of her state Telangana and made her country proud. This is the pinnacle of refreshed thinking and stepping out of one's comfort zone. Are you ready to follow Poorna on her journey to the world’s highest peak?

Raman Roy

There are those who create products. Then there are those who start companies. But then there is the Father of the BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) industry - none other than the legendary pioneer Mr. Raman Roy. Creator of 35,000 jobs and employer of 700,000 people, he has paved a road of opportunities which the youth of this country are following. Now the owner of the billionaire company Quattro, Roy will take you on a journey on the 26th of August 2016 at the first ever TEDx of The Heritage School Gurgaon

Rohit Bansal

Rohit Bansal cofounded Snapdeal along with his friend Kunal Bahl. What started off as a simple coupon booklet business soon transpired into a multi-billion dollar e-commerce, all with a little bit of vision, passion, and perseverance. They noticed a gap in the Indian marketplace, a gap which they endeavoured to fill after being inspired by other companies from other countries. They took advantage of the emerging technology and started Snapdeal. It was a gamble, as pointed out by their peers and investors, and often it didn’t work out how they would have hoped, even leading them to go bankrupt weeks before Rohit’s wedding. Yet these were just hurdles on the track which they learnt how to jump over. Come and discover Rohit Bansal’s journey and learn how he impacted e-commerce in India and what he learnt along the way.

Dr. Nandini C Singh

Nandini Chatterjee Singh is a cognitive neuroscientist at the National Brain Research Centre. Her laboratory works on brain circuits for Language, Literacy and Music. She is passionate about achieving universal literacy by 2020. Her research shows that learning in the native language is the key to functional literacy. Her research also focuses on emotion and music. She also studies the rasas underlying different ragas and their possible role in communicating with children on the autism spectrum.

Sandhya Puchalapalli

Sandhya Puchalapalli has changed the lives of many women in our country. She believes in empowering them to become independent so that they can make their own decisions and don’t have to be dependent on others. To make that possible Sandhya joined hands to form Aarti Home and is its Founder and President. Aarti home provides shelter and education for abandoned girl children to make them economically self-reliant and secures their future. She graduated from GWLN leadership program in 2008 and received the ‘Leader of Impact’ award in 2012 for her initiatives. Sandhya will enlighten you with her motives and what goes behind in making such an initiative possible on the 26th of August 2016 at the very first TEDx of The Heritage School, Gurgaon.

Vinita Singh

Vinita is Managing Trustee of We the People; a part time Development Consultant and a full time active citizen. She is a deep believer in democracy and the humanist values embedded in our Constitution. Her main focus, over the last 20 years, has been to build people’s capacities to uphold their rights and practice responsibilities within our constitutional framework. Through We the People, she is working passionately towards getting citizens more active, informed and engaged in civic issues and thereby reclaiming their role and power. Vinita has an education in Management from the SP Jain Institute in Mumbai and in Social Policy from the London School of Economics.

About The Heritage School

The Heritage School was established in the year 2002 by a great visionary Mr. NC Jain. The vision of education at The Heritage School spreads far beyond the classroom. Learning here takes place in many shapes and forms in the sporting field, on the stage, through managing complex events, leadership activities, outbound expeditions and students clubs to name a few. Our school is one of the largest in Gurgaon, with robust infrastructure, a staff of over 300 and a student population of 3000 students. Renowned surveys have ranked us the best school in NCR and 8th in the country in academics and beyond.
